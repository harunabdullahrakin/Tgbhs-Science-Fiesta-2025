About TGBHS Science Fiesta Join us for a full day of scientific wonder and excitement at TGBHS! Experience hands-on experiments, fascinating demonstrations, competitions, and interactive exhibits spanning all scientific disciplines. This celebration brings together students, teachers, and the community to explore the wonders of science, inspire creativity, and ignite a passion for innovation.

Whether you’re a budding scientist or just curious about how things work, the TGBHS Science Fiesta offers something for everyone to learn, discover, and enjoy.
