TGBHS Science Fiesta

informative

TGBHS Science Fiesta is an exciting science-based event organized by Thakurgaon Government Boys' High School. It brings together students to explore, showcase, and compete in various science-related activities such as projects, experiments, quizzes, and exhibitions, encouraging innovation, creativity, and scientific thinking among young minds.

Registration Guide

Fundamental

Get ready to showcase your passion for science at the TGBHS Science Fiesta! Registration is quick and simple.

Follow the guide below to complete your registration:


  • Registration Fee: ৳100 (One Hundred Taka)

  • Required Info: Full name, school name, class, phone number (email optional)

  • Payment Methods: bKash, Nagad, Rocket, bank transfer, debit/credit cards

  • Platform: Registration will be done via EventSpark

  • Confirmation: You'll receive a confirmation message after successful registration

Make sure to keep your transaction ID or receipt as proof of payment. We look forward to your participation!
